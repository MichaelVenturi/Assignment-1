St. Jude's

St. Jude's Children Hospital spearheads the fight against childhood cancer. St. Jude Children's Research Hospital is located in Memphis, Tennessee and is one of the largest cancer research centers. Most of their research efforts centralize around the idea of understanding the molecular, genetic, and chemical bases of catastrophic diseases in children. They work hard at finding cures for such diseases and promoting their prevention. By donating, you can help put an end to childhood cancer. Click here to donate!

GoFundMe

GoFundMe is the largest fundraising platform, where people can construct campaigns for any case through donations. GoFundMe provides an easy way to raise money for your family, friends and even online community. This platform makes it simple to advertise your campaign in many ways to encourage donations, track your personal progress and post updates to inform your community. Click here to start a campaign!

Toys for Tots

Toys for Tots is a program run by the United States Marine Corps Reserve that administers toys to children whose parents cannot afford to buy them gifts for Christams. This organization has been around sice 1947, distributing 584 million toys and supporting over 260 million children. Some of its accolades include 2003 Outstanding Nonprofit Organization of the Year as well as Reader's Digest Best Children's Charity of 2003. Click here to donate!
